• Livermore Annual Brew Crawl
Experience Livermore's growing Craft Beer scene among the bustling nightlife of Downtown Livermore. Join us as downtown restaurants, tap rooms and other watering holes, showcase the beers of our cities 4 great Breweries. Altamont Beer Works, Eight Bridges Brewing, Shadow Puppet Brewing Company and Working Man Brewing Company will be taking over the taps in Downtown Livermore!
